Projects

Coin Complex Portfolio

The Coin Complex Portfolio app is a market tracking app written in React Native for both IOS and Android. It allows you to track both current and historical price data for all cryptocurrencies, create a watchlist of various cryptocurrencies, and make a portfolio to track its value over time. After investing in cryptocurrencies on various exchanges I realized how difficult it is to keep track of your portfolio value over time, since your tokens are spread across many different offline wallets and exchanges. This mobile app aims to make your tracking simple and easy, even if you have cryptocurrencies spread across many wallets!

Coin Complex Exchange

The Coin Complex Exchange is a cryptocurrency exchange aimed at giving users more data regarding their portfolio and holdings, as well as a better UI for trading. I worked primarily on the frontend which is made with React, D3, and Redux. An open beta for the exchange will be launched soon as the minimum viable product is nearing completion!
